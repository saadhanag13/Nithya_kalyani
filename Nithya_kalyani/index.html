About Us

Started in 2014 by Young Engineerings and experienced workers who diversified into the execution of Residential project development, concentrating mostly in the suburban locations of Chennai and had completed several housing projects and multi-story buildings in Chennai and emerged as one of the trusted names in the field of construction. Catering to all segments comprising Low, Middle & High-income group of society, the Group has earned a name for itself as a dependable Builder amongst the competitors & minds of the public. The Group is now more focused on providing budgeted homes to society and has been very successful in implementing cost-effective methods & providing low-cost solutions to own their dream home. We are a team of Architects, Structural Engineers, construction execution Engineers who are committed to giving the best service and value of money to our clients. Our extreme efforts and intelligence allow us to design and construct Residential, Commercial, Education Institutions, Hospitals, Independent Villas, Row Houses, Industrial Buildings, Plan approval, and many more. Our projects are known for their innovative design, comfort, and sustainability. We have completed nearly 9 projects and 3 on-going projects, all these projects are G+2 projects. we are doing a building plan with vasthu,3D elevation, septic tank all free of cost. Our objective is to provide our clients with an "I AM ASSURED" experience when we are chosen to execute their projects. we emphasize clear communication and follow thorough procedures that ensure that our client's objectives are met in the planning and execution of all our projects.
